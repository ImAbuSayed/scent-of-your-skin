[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
The scent of your skin is a memory I keep
Like a warm, gentle fire as I drift off to sleep
There's a pull in your eyes when the daylight has gone
A quiet attraction that lingers till dawn

[Verse 1]
You walked through the room with a smile on your face
And my heart caught a rhythm, a steady embrace
The air turned to honey, the world slowed on down
With you as the only sweet sound in this town

[Pre-Chorus]
[Whispered]
(Come a little closer now)
The shadows are long and the moment is right
There’s a spark in the dark as we lean in tonight

[Chorus]
Oh, the scent of your skin is a map I will read
Every gentle breath drawn is the whisper I need
I am lost in the motion, the touch of your hand
A slow-burning fire that I understand

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, I need it, I need it, the warmth of your skin
I want it, I want it, let the midnight begin

[Verse 2]

There’s a beautiful promise that shines in your eye
I trace every line that your silhouette makes
And savor the shiver my tender touch wakes

[Bridge]
